1. Introduction

This project aims to identify the types of apps that attract more users.
To reach this objective we will need to collect and analyze data about mobile apps available on App Store.

2. Exploring the dataset content

We will use the IOS App store existing dataset for this project, the App Store contains 7197 data rows and 16 columns, and the data was collected in July 2017. The dataset can be downloaded directly from this link[https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/ramamet4/app-store-apple-data-set-10k-apps]

The dataset was opened and explored using Microsoft Excel office, and several variables were noticed including those not needed for this analysis. And from looking around it can be concluded that the needed variables or columns for this analysis are 'track name, price, rating count total, rating count version, user ratings, and prime genre'.

3. Data cleaning

Before the analysis was carried out, we need to make sure there are no wrong information or mistakes in the data to avoid making wrong conclusions in the analysis.

Some of the tasks checked and carried out include the following:

- duplicate data
- non-English app names
- paid and free apps
- inaccurate data ( data with different numbers of columns)

After careful scrolling and checking of the dataset, the tasks that were carried out include:
 
- Removal of duplicate data
- Removal of Non-English app names
- Separation of paid and free apps
- Removal of Inaccurate data ( data with wrong column data)

After the removal of inaccurate data from the dataset, there were duplicate entries that were otherwise not noticed upon scanning for duplicate rows initially, 13 duplicate rows were removed from the dataset.

Another point to note for the duplicated rows is that the rows deleted are lesser or equal to the rating count total.

After cleaning, the total is now 6096 of which the total data for free apps is now 3172 and that of paid apps is 2923.
